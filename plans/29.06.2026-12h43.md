# ☠️ The Sythanis Post-Mortem

> *An age of brief lives, fierce loves, and blood-soaked soil — where jealousy was the engine and violence the exit.*

---

## Executive Summary

- **40 founders seeded a world** that exploded to **1,336 souls** across 1,296 births; only **469 survived**.
- **89% of all deaths were violent** — 772 killings in a society of passionate, jealous lovers who turned murder into their leading cause of death.
- **Every single death by violence was a "crime of passion"** — no cold murders, no wars — only love-triangle killings.
- **1,123 disease infections** swept through the population (Fever, Plague, Malaria, Typhus), yet **only 38 died of disease** — a 92% cure rate.
- **Mean age at death was 23.5** (median: 16); those who survived to old age lived to **100+ on average**, dying of cancer.
- **653 couples formed** in a hyper-romantic society — **61,346 Dates** and **42,198 Flirts** recorded.
- **Three famous dynasties** produced **45 children each** — Gaebeothax & Riaxlukra, founders of the great clans.

## 🔍 Closing Reflection

Sythanis was a society defined by **intensity** — intensely social (166,952 actions, 61,346 dates), intensely reproductive (1,296 births, dynasties of 45 children), and intensely violent (89% of deaths by murder). The romance that bound couples together was the same force that tore them apart: jealousy, love triangles, and crimes of passion were not exceptions but **the engine of mortality**. In a world where the median lifespan was 16 and almost everyone died at someone else's hand, the survivors were those who somehow navigated the razor's edge between love and lethal jealousy — or those who, like the cancer victims, simply outlived everyone who wanted them dead.

## 📊 Methodology & Caveats

- **Records parsed:** 6 log files (cmd_log.txt, births_log.txt, deaths_log.txt, actions_log.txt, diseases_log.txt, relationships_log.txt, events_log.txt)
- **Deaths deduplicated:** 867 unique death IDs found, 867 death lines — **0% collision rate** (perfect deduplication)
- **Age data available for:** 867 of 867 deaths (100%)
- **Violence vs. events discrepancy:** The events_log records only 1 crime_of_passion event and 1 jealousy episode, while deaths_log records 772 crime of passion deaths. This likely means the jealousy/crime_of_passion event system logs only a subset of narrative highlights, while the internal simulation generates deaths from passion-driven violence at a much higher rate without logging each emotional precursor.
- **Founder identification:** Founders identified as entities appearing in cmd_log with `Personality` data. IDs below 20000 were treated as founders for the founder-death analysis, confirmed by the personality extraction.
- **Disease deaths:** Only 3 of 38 "disease" deaths are from named infectious diseases (Malaria, Typhus, Fever). The remaining 33 are "illness (cancer)" — a separate category that bypasses the cure system.
